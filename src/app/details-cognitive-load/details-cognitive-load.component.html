Allscripts UX Poster Campaign

Our team's poster campaign attempted to educate Allscripts employees about the basic principles of design, user experience, and human factors. There were about 12 posters total, each with a different theme, with each person on the team choosing the theme for and creating their own poster. These were posted on walls in the company offices.

I chose the theme of Cognitive Tax.

When printed, the posters were approximately 4 feet tall.
