The CareQuality application was a new product for internal configuration and management of customers on the CareQuality network.

The development team chose to use the UI starter kit that I created and that I also maintain.

As this was the first team that I worked closely with on the starter kit, I learned of the ways that a team would actually use the kit in a product. I tailored the starter kit to be more streamlined with UI development. For example, bugs are inevitable, and getting an update to the development team should be as seamless as possible.
