The CareQuality application was a new product for internal configuration and management of customers on the CareQuality network.

The development team chose to use the UI Starter Kit that I created and that I also maintain.

As this was the first team that I worked closely with on the starter kit, I learned how a team would actually use the kit in a product. So, in parallel with the UI design work I was doing, I was also improving and streamlining the UI Starter Kit to better fit developers' usage of it.
